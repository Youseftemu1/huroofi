Huroofi (حروفي) is an educational app for teaching Arabic letters to children. It features interactive drag-and-drop functionality, multiple categories (Animals, Objects, Nature), text-to-speech, and audio support.

Features:
• 28 Arabic letters (ا to ي)
• 3 categories: Animals, Objects, Nature
• Interactive drag-and-drop
• Text-to-speech for Arabic pronunciation
• Beautiful animations and responsive design
• RTL (Right-to-Left) support

Perfect for children learning Arabic letters in a fun and interactive way!
