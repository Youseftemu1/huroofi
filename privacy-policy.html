Privacy Policy for Huroofi

Last updated: January 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Huroofi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Huroofi (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

Huroofi does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App operates entirely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track user activity
  • Store user information
  • Connect to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Request internet permissions

3. Local Storage

The App may store data locally on your device (such as app preferences) for the sole purpose of improving your experience. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Third-Party Services

Huroofi does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information. The App uses:

  • Text-to-Speech (TTS): Operates locally on your device, no data is sent externally
  • Audio Playback: Plays local audio files stored in the app, no network access required

5. Children's Privacy

Huroofi is designed for children and families. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the App does not collect any data, there is no risk of collecting children's information.

6. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Audio permissions: To play educational sounds and pronunciations (used locally only)

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

7. Data Security

Since Huroofi does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the App at any time, which will remove all locally stored data.
